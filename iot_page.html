The objective of this activity was to design an ideal elevator, meeting a set of conditions and using Iot to meet these conditions from the point of view of the company. These are purely theoretical but can provide a template for a company that may want to look into this.

#2. IoT Guest Lecture

Our lecture hosted an enthralling guest lecture centered around the remarkable potential of Arduino. The guest lecturer shared his wealth of knowledge about Arduino, captivating the audience with his passion and expertise. He delved into the vast applications of Arduino in various fields, from traffic lights to irrigation systems. He demonstrated how this open-source electronics platform can empower individuals to bring their creative ideas to life. The lecture concluded with an interactive session, where attendees had the chance to ask questions and gain practical insights into Arduino projects and get familiar with the various components.
